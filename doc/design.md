# Dream-Room
Dream Room is a game where you find yourself in a very dream-like room with no idea how or why you got there.

Everything within the room is designed with the goal in mind to make it as dream-like as possible. Meaning that it should have a whimsical, abstract feeling though still have some sort of meaning to gleam from in the end.

Within the room is five objects that the player needs to interact with and complete to figure out the story of the room and complete the game. Some objects unlock others which will give the game an escape room feeling.
